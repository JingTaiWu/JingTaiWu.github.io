About Me

I am a senior studying Computer Science at Marist College in Poughkeepsie, New York and currently interning at Software Consulting Associates in Red Hook.

A Little History

I was born and raised in Shanghai, China. My family moved to Honolulu in 2005. At the time, I just finished elementary school in China, so I was a bit nervous about going to American schools. Fortunately throughout middle school and high school, I had amazing teachers who helped me overcome the difficulties.

Why New York?

People that I meet in New York tend to ask the one common question: Why did you come all the way from Hawaii to New York? It's a paradise there!

Hawaii is a paradise. That's true, but people usually forget that it is also a very small state. There are only a few things to do here and it gets boring very easily. When I was applying for colleges during senior year of high school, I reminded myself to only target schools outside of Hawaii and I am fortunate enough to find Marist College.
